Where we've Been

Since I was elected Chairman, we've had some great successes. A few examples are:

Obtained ballot access and party recognition in Oklahoma for the first time since 2000.

Adoption of a new logo and professional branding for the Libertarian Party.

Fundraising for and hiring Andy Burns to focus full-time on developing state affiliates.

Acted as a spokesman for the party in scores of television and other media interviews.

Travelled to state conventions all over the country to speak to Libertarians.

Ending the infighting and factionalism that used to plague the Libertarian National Committee.

Where We're Going

  • Providing a strong voice for the Libertarian Party on the national stage.
  • Fighting in states like Ohio to roll back restrictive ballot access laws.
  • Assisting more state affiliate parties in developing their organizational capabilities.
  • Developing issue campaigns and other efforts to prospect to people who are not Libertarian yet.
